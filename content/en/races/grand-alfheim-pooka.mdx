<Section icon={Target} title="Plaza Blacksmith & Equipment Customization">

The blacksmith located in the eastern wing of the Pooka plaza is the cornerstone of character progression in this region. Unlike basic merchants, the blacksmith provides specialized services that allow players to refine their weapons, swap classes, and upgrade gear tiers. Investing resources into weapon upgrades is the most efficient way to scale your damage output, especially when preparing for high-level content.

Swapping weapons at the blacksmith allows you to test different playstyles. For example, transitioning from a fast-paced Duelist build to a heavy-hitting Titan Blade build changes your attack animations, reach, and defensive capabilities.
