<Section icon={Map} title="Salamander Territory and Leveling Route">

Progressing as a **grand alfheim salamander** involves utilizing your home territory's unique resources and boss encounters. The Salamander region is not just a starting zone; it is a hub for essential mid-game equipment and the primary entrance to the game's first major dungeon.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Forge and First Quests">
    Start in the Salamander capital. Immediately visit the Blacksmith to select your starter class (Titanblade or Swordsman recommended). Complete all local delivery and hunting quests to reach level 5.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Satyr Boss Farming">
    Once you reach level 8, locate the **Satyr** miniboss in the Salamander wilds. This boss is the primary source for the **Bloodcrown Regalia Set**. Farm this boss until you have the full set for a major HP boost.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Temple of Beginnings">
    At level 10, head to the Salamander entrance of the **Temple of Beginnings**. Choose the "Monster Route" to maximize your Fire Magic AoE potential and farm for the **Night's Edge** sword or **Azure Knightplate** armor.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Expansion to Other Lands">
    After clearing the Bone Dragon at level 15-20, use your wings to fly toward the Gnome or Undine territories. Continue your main questline while upgrading your Fire Magic to the "Ignition" tier.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The Satyr boss is particularly important because it drops the **Iron Boarding Axe**, a powerful early-game STR weapon that can carry a Salamander through the first 15 levels with ease.

| Boss | Location | Recommended Level | Key Drops |
|------|----------|-------------------|-----------|

| **Treant** | Temple (Interior) | 12-15 | Verdant Sovereign Set |
| **Bone Dragon** | Temple (End) | 15+ | Night's Edge, Obsidian Requiem |
