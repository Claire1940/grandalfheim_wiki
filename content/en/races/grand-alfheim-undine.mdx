<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Magic Affinity">

Combat in Grand Alfheim is a sophisticated system of blocks, parries, and skill rotations. For a **grand alfheim undine** player, mastering the timing of Water Magic is essential. Unlike fire magic, which focuses on damage over time, water magic often involves slowing enemies or restoring the health of allies. This requires a high level of battlefield awareness, as you must track both the enemy's movements and your teammates' health bars.
