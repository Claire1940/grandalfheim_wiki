<Section icon={Map} title="Exploration and Treasure Hunting Perks">

One of the most unique aspects of the **grand alfheim spriggan** is their passive specialty in **treasure-seeking**. While exploring large race territories, dungeons, and hidden ruins, Spriggans possess an enhanced ability to locate valuable chests and hidden rewards.

This makes them the premier race for farming rare materials and equipment. When combined with their high-speed flight mechanics, Spriggans can navigate vertical environments and reach hidden ledges faster than heavier races like Gnomes.

### Key Exploration Areas for Spriggans

- **Temple of Beginnings**: Locate hidden chests along the left parkour route.
- **Salamander Territory**: Efficiently scout open-world areas to locate the Satyr miniboss.
- **Gnome Territory**: Navigate the revamped map terrain easily to find the entrance of the level 50 dungeon, Ode to the Abyss.
