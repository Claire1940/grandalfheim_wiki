<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Control Schemes">

Grand Alfheim features a skill-based combat system that rewards timing over mindless button mashing. Whether you are playing on PC, Mobile, or Console, mastering the defensive layers—blocking, parrying, and dashing—is mandatory for surviving dungeons.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Offensive Pressure">
    - **M1 Combos**: Standard weapon strings.
    - **Criticals (R)**: Heavy hits to break guards.
    - **Feints (M2)**: Cancel attacks to bait parries.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Defensive Layering">
    - **Blocking (F)**: Reduces incoming damage.
    - **Parrying**: Time F perfectly to stun enemies.
    - **Dashing (Q)**: I-frames to dodge AOE attacks.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Aerial Mobility">
    - **Flight (V)**: Consume mana to fly.
    - **Dash Attack**: Perform Q then M1 for a gap closer.
    - **Air Combo**: Jump then M1 to keep targets airborne.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

The following table summarizes the essential PC keybinds you need to memorize before your first duel.

| Action | Keybind | Usage Context |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Attack** | M1 / Left Click | Primary damage source |
| **Block / Parry** | F | Hold to block / Tap to parry |
| **Dash / Dodge** | Q | Avoiding heavy boss swings |
| **Critical Hit** | R | Breaking enemy stamina |
| **Feint** | M2 / Right Click | Tricking PvP opponents |
| **Fly** | V | Exploration and verticality |
| **Mount** | H | Ground travel between towns |
| **Menu** | M | Equipment and Skill Tree |

<Callout type="warning" title="Stamina Management">
Every action, including blocking and dashing, consumes stamina. If your stamina hits zero, you will be Guard Broken, leaving you vulnerable to a full combo. Never spam your M1 string until empty!
</Callout>

<Section icon={Zap} title="Step-by-Step Leveling and Progression Route">

To progress efficiently, you should follow the main questline while weaving in dungeon runs for gear upgrades. Avoid grinding low-level mobs; quests provide significantly more Experience and Currency (Yrd).

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Tutorial & First Weapon">
    Complete the initial tutorial prompts. Immediately head to the nearest town's Blacksmith to claim your free starter weapon. This defines your initial class.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Starter Quests (Lvl 1-10)">
    Follow the main story markers. Accept side quests from NPCs with exclamation marks. Focus on upgrading your **Magic Skill Tree** alongside your weapon skills.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Temple of Beginnings (Lvl 10-20)">
    Enter your first dungeon. You can choose the **Parkour Route** (faster) or the **Combat Route** (more XP). Defeat the **Bone Dragon** to farm your first Epic and Legendary gear sets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Territory Expansion (Lvl 20-50)">
    Travel to other race territories (Salamander, Gnome, etc.). Defeat world bosses like the **Satyr** to obtain high-HP armor sets like the **Bloodcrown Regalia**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Endgame: Ode to the Abyss (Lvl 50+)">
    Join a group for the level 50 raid. This dungeon only opens at specific times (12:00 AM/PM ET). Success here grants **Mythical** weapons like the **Voidrune Edge**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Expert Leveling Tip">
Always keep food in your inventory. The hunger system affects your health regeneration; eating cooked meals ensures you don't have to retreat from combat to heal constantly.
</Callout>

</Section>
