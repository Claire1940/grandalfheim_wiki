<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Leveling and Progression Route">

To level up efficiently, you must combine main story quests, local side quests, and targeted dungeon farming. Following a structured route ensures you are always properly geared for the challenges ahead.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Tutorial & Starter Quests (Lv. 1-5)">
    Complete the full tutorial zone, choose your initial class at the forge, and equip your starter weapon. Focus on the main quests immediately outside the starting town to gain early levels and currency.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Open-World Training & Magic (Lv. 5-9)">
    Follow the questlines through your race's starting territory. Invest your early skill points into a small, reliable class rotation and begin developing your racial magic tree to mix physical and magical attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Temple of Beginnings (Lv. 10-19)">
    Travel to the Temple of Beginnings. Run the dungeon repeatedly to replace your common blacksmith gear with Rare and Epic sets like the Azure Knightplate.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Territory Exploration & Bosses (Lv. 20-49)">
    Explore neighboring racial territories, complete advanced side quests, and farm open-world minibosses like the Satyr to obtain high-tier armor sets and weapon drops.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Endgame Ode to the Abyss (Lv. 50+)">
    Form a coordinated party of 4 to 8 players and enter Ode to the Abyss. Master the multi-phase boss fight to obtain Mythical endgame weapons and armor.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Progression Milestone">
Always prioritize class-specific quests when they become available. These quests provide massive chunks of experience and often unlock essential active skills in your weapon's tree.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Dungeon Guides: Temple of Beginnings to Ode to the Abyss">

Dungeons are the primary source of high-tier equipment in Grand Alfheim. These instances combine environmental puzzles, dangerous mob pulls, and multi-phase boss encounters that test your team coordination.

| Dungeon Name | Level Req. | Key Mechanics | Notable Loot Drops |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Temple of Beginnings** | Level 10+ | Parkour or combat routes, angel/demon cube puzzles, Bone Dragon boss | Azure Knightplate Set, Night's Edge, Obsidian Requiem, Crimson Dragon Set |
| **Ode to the Abyss** | Level 50+ | Elevator descent, Valdrayn boss, 4-tower activation phase at low health | Voidrune Edge, Nightreaver Bow, Voidfang Gauntlets, Abyssal Covenant |

### Dungeon Strategies

When running the **Temple of Beginnings**, groups can split up to handle the cube puzzles faster. Placing the cubes on the matching angel and demon altars is required to open the portal to the Bone Dragon. During the final fight, ensure your party spreads out so the dragon's breath attacks do not wipe the entire group at once.

For **Ode to the Abyss**, coordination is mandatory. When the boss reaches its low-health threshold, four towers will activate around the arena. Your team must assign specific players to destroy these towers quickly, or the boss will regenerate health and wipe the raid.
