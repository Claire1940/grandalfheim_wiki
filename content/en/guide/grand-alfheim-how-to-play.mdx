<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering the Controls">

Effective combat in Grand Alfheim relies on more than just spamming the M1 button. The game utilizes a "stamina-based" combat system where blocking, dashing, and using skills all consume resources. Learning the timing of a parry can turn a difficult boss fight into a manageable encounter.

| Action | PC Keybind | Mobile/Console | Usage Tip |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Attack** | M1 (Left Click) | Attack Button | Stop the string early to avoid counters |
| **Feint** | M2 (Right Click) | Feint Button | Use to bait an enemy's block or parry |
| **Block / Parry** | F | Shield Icon | Press 'F' just before impact to parry |
| **Dash** | Q | Dash Button | Use to avoid heavy area-of-effect (AoE) |
| **Flight** | V | Wing Icon | Double jump or press V to glide/fly |
| **Critical** | R | Skill Button | Only use when an enemy is recovering |
| **Mount** | H | Horse Icon | Faster ground travel between towns |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Practice the M1-M1-M1-M2 Combo">
    Start a standard attack string but end it with a feint to confuse opponents in PvP or break the guard of humanoid NPCs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Perfect the Parry">
    Don't hold block indefinitely; your guard will break. Watch the enemy's animation and tap 'F' at the last second to stun them.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Stamina Management">
    Always keep at least 20% of your stamina bar full. If you hit 0%, you will be unable to dash or parry, leaving you vulnerable to a critical hit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Input Lag">
If you are playing on high-ping servers, you may need to parry slightly earlier than the animation suggests. Adjust your timing based on the visual feedback of your hits.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Dungeons and Boss Mechanics">

Dungeons are the primary source of high-tier equipment in Grand Alfheim. The first major challenge is the **Temple of Beginnings**, which introduces puzzles and multi-phase boss fights.

**Key Dungeon: Temple of Beginnings**
- **Entry**: Level 10+ recommended.
- **Routes**: Choose the **Left Path** for parkour (faster) or the **Right Path** for combat (more XP).
- **Puzzle**: You must place cubes on the matching altars to open the boss portal.
- **Boss (Bone Dragon)**: Avoid standing directly in front of the dragon. When it prepares its breath attack, dash to the sides and punish its recovery.
