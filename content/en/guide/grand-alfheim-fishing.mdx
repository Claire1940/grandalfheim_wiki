<Section icon={Target} title="How to Master the Reeling Minigame">

The core of grand alfheim fishing lies in its interactive minigame. Unlike passive fishing systems in other RPGs, you must actively fight the fish's pull by observing the physical lean of your fishing rod and inputting the correct counter-movements on your keyboard.

| Rod Direction | Required Key Press | Reeling Status |
|--- |--- |--- |
| Leaning Right | Press **D** | Pulling Left |
| Leaning Left | Press **A** | Pulling Right |
| Centered | Press **S** / Neutral | Reeling In |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Cast Your Line">
    Equip your fishing rod and interact with any viable water source, such as the docks in Salamander Town. Wait quietly for the fish to bite.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Observe the Tension">
    Watch the physical angle of your fishing rod. Do not look for a traditional UI bar; instead, focus on which way the rod bends.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Counter-Steer to Reel">
    If the rod pulls to the right, hold **D**. If it pulls to the left, hold **A**. This brings the fishing rod closer to your character.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Claim Your Catch">
    Once the fish is pulled all the way in, the catch menu will automatically pop up, displaying your fish type and awarded experience.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Watch Your Keys">
Pressing the wrong key when the rod leans will increase tension, causing the fish to escape. Always react to the physical movement of the rod rather than guessing.
</Callout>

</Section>
