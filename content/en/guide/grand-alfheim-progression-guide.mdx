<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Leveling Route">

To reach the endgame efficiently, you must follow a structured leveling path. Spreading your efforts across too many high-level zones early on will slow down your progression.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Tutorial & Class Setup (Level 1-5)">
    Complete the initial tutorial to master basic movement. Learn how to run, roll, block, and fly (V). Visit the forge immediately to select your starting class and claim your free starter weapon.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Starter Quests (Level 5-9)">
    Follow the main questline through your starting race territory. Complete any nearby side quests to accumulate experience, currency, and skill points. Invest early skill points into your core class abilities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Temple of Beginnings (Level 10-19)">
    Travel to the Temple of Beginnings. Run this starter dungeon repeatedly to replace your starter gear with Epic and Legendary drops. Practice parrying the Treant miniboss and dodging the Bone Dragon's area attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Territory Bosses (Level 20-49)">
    Expand your exploration into neighboring race territories. Complete high-reward regional quests and farm open-world minibosses like the Satyr in Salamander territory to secure mid-game armor upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Ode to the Abyss (Level 50+)">
    Form a coordinated party of 4 to 8 players. Enter the level 50 endgame dungeon, Ode to the Abyss, during its scheduled 10-minute opening window. Defeat the final boss to obtain Mythical weapons.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Dungeon Entry Times">
The endgame dungeon **Ode to the Abyss** is located in Gnome territory. Its entrance only opens twice a day at **12:00 AM** and **12:00 PM Eastern Time**. Make sure your party is gathered at the gate 5 minutes before opening.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Combat Mechanics & Controls">

Grand Alfheim features an active combat system that rewards precise timing, parries, and positioning over mindless button-mashing. Memorizing the core keybinds is crucial for both PvE farming and PvP encounters.

### Official PC Keybinds

| Action | Input | Combat Application |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Attack** | M1 / Left Click | Executes standard weapon attack strings. |
| **Feint** | M2 / Right Click | Cancels an active attack to bait out enemy blocks. |
| **Block / Parry** | F | Hold to block damage. Tap right before a hit to parry. |
| **Dash** | Q | Quick repositioning. Follow with M1 for a Dash Attack. |
| **Critical Attack** | R | Heavy shield-breaking or guard-breaking strike. |
| **Flight** | V | Activates racial wings for vertical exploration. |
