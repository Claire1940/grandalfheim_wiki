<Section icon={Map} title="Introduction to Grand Alfheim Cooking and Survival">

The Phase 6 update transformed the open world of Grand Alfheim by introducing crucial survival mechanics: hunger and cooking. Players can no longer rely solely on their class gear and magic skill trees to survive tough encounters. Managing your hunger meter is now just as important as upgrading your weapons. 

When your character goes hungry, your overall stamina regeneration drops significantly, making it difficult to dodge, block, or execute continuous weapon combos. Cooking transforms raw ingredients harvested from the wild or dropped by monsters into nutritious meals that restore your hunger bar and grant temporary stat boosts.

| Survival Metric | Buff Impact | Debuff Impact | Best Recovery Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hunger Meter** | Enhanced stamina regen, bonus stats | Reduced stamina, slower movement | Eat cooked tier meals |
| **Stamina Bar** | Faster combo execution, continuous flight | Inability to dodge or block | Keep hunger above 50% |

<Callout type="warning" title="Hunger Warning">
Do not let your hunger bar drop to zero. A completely depleted hunger meter slowly drains your health and locks you out of using high-stamina class skills, leaving you highly vulnerable during boss fights.
</Callout>

</Section>

Gnome territory is the premier zone for gathering vegetables and mushrooms due to its major map revamp in Phase 6. For high-quality meats, hunting wild beasts and monsters in the starter zones and mid-game territories is the most reliable path.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Cooking Station Locations & Crafting Steps">

Cooking cannot be done on the fly; you must locate a designated cooking station or campfire. These stations are typically found in major racial towns, safe outposts, and near dungeon entrances. The steps to prepare a meal are straightforward but require precision to avoid wasting valuable ingredients.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate a Cooking Station">
    Travel to a major town or find a campfire in Gnome, Salamander, or Undine territory. Approach the cooking pot and press the interaction key to open the cooking interface.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Your Recipe">
    Browse your unlocked recipes in the cooking menu. Ensure you have all the required raw meats, vegetables, and seasoning agents in your inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Combine the Ingredients">
    Place the correct ratio of ingredients into the cooking slots. Mixing incorrect items will result in a ruined dish that provides minimal hunger restoration.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Cook Action">
    Confirm the action to begin cooking. Once complete, the cooked meal will be added directly to your inventory, ready for consumption or trading.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Dungeon Preparation">
Always cook a batch of high-tier meals before entering the Temple of Beginnings or Ode to the Abyss. Eating a meal right before the boss portal ensures your buffs last throughout the entire encounter.
</Callout>

</Section>
