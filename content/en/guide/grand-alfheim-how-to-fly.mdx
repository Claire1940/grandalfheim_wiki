<Section icon={Shield} title="Flight Stamina and Survival Mechanics">

The most critical aspect of the **grand alfheim how to fly** system is the flight stamina bar. Represented as a **yellow icon** in the top-left corner of your HUD, this resource dictates how long you can remain airborne. If this meter reaches zero, your character will automatically stall and fall. Depending on your altitude, this can result in significant fall damage or even instant death.

Stamina management is a skill in itself. It regenerates automatically whenever you are standing on solid ground or swimming, but it will not recover while you are hovering or moving in the air.

| Stamina State | Icon Color | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Full / High** | Bright Yellow | Maximum maneuverability and speed. |
| **Low / Critical** | Flashing Yellow | Character begins to wobble; prepare for landing. |
| **Depleted** | Greyed Out | Immediate forced descent and fall damage risk. |
| **Regenerating** | Pulsing | Occurs only when grounded; rate depends on stats. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Danger: Fall Damage">
Always keep at least 10% of your stamina bar as a "reserve" for landing. If you run out of stamina high above the ground, you won't be able to reactivate flight to break your fall.
</Callout>
