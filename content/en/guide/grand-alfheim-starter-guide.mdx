To level up efficiently and prepare your character for difficult endgame content, follow this structured roadmap from your very first login.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete the Tutorial">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Your Starter Weapon">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Follow Main & Side Quests">
    Complete quests in your starting territory to quickly reach level 10. Avoid fighting high-level monsters early on; stick to enemies close to your current level.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Upgrade Your Skill Trees">
    Invest your earned points into a few highly reliable class and magic skills. Do not spread your points too thin across too many different branches.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Combat Controls Quick Reference">

Navigating combat successfully requires understanding your keybinds and defensive options.

| Action | PC Input | Mobile Input | Combat Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Basic Attack** | M1 / Left Click | Touch HUD Button | Standard combo string |
| **Feint Attack** | M2 / Right Click | Touch HUD Button | Cancel attacks to bait blocks |
| **Block / Parry** | F | Touch HUD Button | Hold to block; tap to parry |
| **Dash** | Q | Touch HUD Button | Dodge attacks or close distance |
| **Fly** | V | Touch HUD Button | Toggle wings for aerial travel |

<Callout type="warning" title="Stamina Management">
Do not deplete your entire stamina bar on offensive moves. Always save enough stamina to perform a dash (Q) or a block (F) when an enemy counters.
</Callout>
