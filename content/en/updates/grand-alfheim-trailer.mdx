Starting your journey in Grand Alfheim requires a balance between learning the controls and efficiently completing the initial quest lines. The following steps will guide you from character creation to your first major dungeon completion.

  <Step num="1" title="Character Creation and Race Selection">

<Section icon={Shield} title="Controls, Combat, and Community">

Mastering the combat system is more than just clicking M1. Grand Alfheim uses a "feint and parry" system that rewards players who can read enemy animations. Using the M2 button to feint can trick opponents into wasting their block, leaving them open for a critical attack.

**Essential PC Controls:**
- **M1**: Basic Attack
- **M2**: Feint Attack
- **F**: Block (Timed for Parry)
- **Q**: Dash / Dash Attack
- **R**: Critical Attack
- **V**: Toggle Flight
- **H**: Summon Mount
