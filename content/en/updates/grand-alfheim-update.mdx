<Section icon={Swords} title="Classes and Starter Weapon Selection">

In Grand Alfheim, your combat style is dictated by your equipped weapon class. Unlike traditional games, you do not lock yourself into a single class permanently. You can visit a **Forge or Blacksmith NPC** at any time to switch your weapon, allowing you to test different roles.

Each class scales with specific stats and requires distinct playstyles to maximize effectiveness in PvE and PvP.

| Class | Weapon Type | Main Stat | Combat Role | Specialty |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Swordsman** | Sword | STR | Balanced Melee | Straightforward combos, highly beginner-friendly. |
| **Bowmaster** | Bow / Ammo | DEX | Ranged DPS | Distance control, charged shots, safe kiting. |
| **Assassin** | Daggers | DEX/STR | Fast Burst | Quick melee strings, high mobility, poison pressure. |
| **Pugilist** | Gauntlets | STR | Close Combo | Rapid close-range strikes, heavy stamina pressure. |
| **Duelist** | Rapier | DEX | Precision DPS | Fast thrusts, spacing, quick counterattacks. |
| **Titanblade** | Greatsword | STR | Heavy Melee | Slow, high-impact swings with massive reach. |
| **Aegis** | Greatshield | STR | Frontline Tank | Blocks, deflects, and absorbs incoming boss damage. |

<Callout type="tip" title="Class Swapping Tip">
If you find a high-rarity weapon drop that doesn't match your current class, simply head back to a Blacksmith. You can equip the new weapon and swap your class skill tree instantly without losing your overall character level progression.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Beginner Progression Route">

To help you navigate the new content introduced in the **grand alfheim update**, follow this optimized progression path. This route ensures you are properly geared before attempting high-level group content.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Character Creation & Race Selection">
    Choose from one of the nine fairy races (such as Salamander for offense, Sylph for speed, or Undine for healing). Customize your appearance and complete the basic movement tutorial.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock Your Starter Class">
    Visit the starting town's Blacksmith. Select your starting class weapon (such as the Iron Sword or Wooden Bow) and practice basic M1 combos, dodging (Q), and parrying (F).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Complete Starter Quests">
    Follow the main questline through your racial territory. Accumulate experience and gold while leveling up both your weapon class skill tree and your racial magic tree.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Conquer the Temple of Beginnings">
    Once you reach **level 10**, head to the Temple of Beginnings. Run the dungeon alone or with a party, solve the cube puzzles, and defeat the Bone Dragon to farm your first Epic and Legendary gear sets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Endgame Preparation">
    Progress toward level 50 by completing high-level territory quests, fighting open-world bosses like the Satyr, and refining your stat allocation for the ultimate group dungeon, **Ode to the Abyss**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Stamina Management">
Do not spam your M1 attacks blindly. Many enemies and bosses have quick counter-attack windows. Always save enough stamina to perform a dash (Q) or a block/parry (F) when an incoming red attack indicator appears.
</Callout>

</Section>
