If you decide to change your fighting style later, simply return to a blacksmith to equip a different weapon type without losing your overall level progression.

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Beginner Progression Route">

To level up efficiently during the **grand alfheim early access** phase, follow this structured progression path. This route guides you from character creation through your first major dungeon clear.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Character Creation & Setup">
    Choose a race that matches your preferred playstyle (e.g., Salamander for DPS, Undine for Support). Customize your appearance, finish the brief opening tutorial to learn basic movement, and open your main menu with the **M key** to inspect your character sheet.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Your Starter Class">
    Locate the nearest blacksmith or forge. Choose your starter weapon class. We highly recommend starting with the **Swordsman** or **Bowmaster** to learn enemy attack animations safely. Equip the free starter weapon provided by the blacksmith.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Complete Main and Side Quests">
    Follow the main questline through your starting territory. Pick up side quests from NPCs along the way to maximize your experience and gold gains. Defeat enemies close to your level; fighting over-leveled monsters slows down your progression speed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Master Flight and Mounts">
    Press the **V key** to activate your racial wings. Practice flying, as vertical navigation is essential for reaching higher-level quest areas. For ground travel across large, flat territories, press the **H key** to summon your equipped mount.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Conquer the Temple of Beginnings">
    Once you reach Level 10, head to the Temple of Beginnings (accessible from Salamander, Undine, and Gnome territories). Choose the left parkour route to avoid trash mobs, or the right combat route for extra experience. Solve the cube puzzle to face the final boss, the **Bone Dragon**, and farm Epic and Legendary gear.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Dungeon Preparation">
Always stock up on healing items before entering the Temple of Beginnings. If you are struggling with the parkour or combat sections, form a party with other players in the area to share boss aggro.
</Callout>

</Section>
