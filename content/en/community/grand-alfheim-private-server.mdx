<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Classes and Weapon Loadouts">

In Grand Alfheim, your class is determined by the weapon you equip. Unlike other RPGs where your class is locked, you can visit a Blacksmith at any time to test a new fighting style. This flexibility is core to the **grand alfheim private server** experience, allowing you to adapt to different boss mechanics on the fly.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Visit the Forge">
    Locate the Blacksmith in the starting town or major racial capitals. You will need a small amount of currency to purchase your first starter weapon.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Your Weapon">
    Choose from classes like Swordsman (Balanced), Bowmaster (Ranged), or Aegis (Tank). Each comes with a unique skill tree that unlocks as you level up.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master the Inputs">
    Practice your M1 (Basic), M2 (Feint), and F (Parry) timing. In Grand Alfheim, a successful parry creates a massive opening for critical attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Allocate Skill Points">
    Open your menu (M key) and invest points into your class tree. Focus on a few core abilities rather than spreading points too thin.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Stamina Management">

Once you reach the level cap in the **grand alfheim private server**, your focus shifts to the "Ode to the Abyss" dungeon. This is currently the most difficult content in the game, requiring a coordinated party of at least four players. The rewards, however, are the only source of Mythical-tier equipment like the **Voidrune Edge** and the **Nightreaver Bow**.

**Dungeon Mechanics: Ode to the Abyss**
- **Entry Window**: The dungeon entrance in Gnome territory only opens at 12:00 AM and 12:00 PM Eastern Time for 10 minutes.
- **The Elevator**: You must descend into Valdrayn using the central elevator.
- **The Tower Phase**: When the boss reaches low health, four towers will activate. You must split your team to destroy all four simultaneously, or the boss will regenerate health.

| Boss | Location | Difficulty | Notable Drops |
