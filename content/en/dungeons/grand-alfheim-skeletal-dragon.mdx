<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Strategy: Defeating the Skeletal Dragon">

The fight against the **grand alfheim skeletal dragon** is divided into two primary phases. In the first phase, the dragon remains grounded, utilizing its massive tail and claws to keep players at bay. In the second phase, it becomes more aggressive, incorporating ground-shaking slams and faster rotation speeds. Success requires a rhythmic approach to attacking and defending.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Positioning and Initial Aggro">
    Enter the arena and allow the tank or the player with the highest defense to draw the first strike. Position yourself at the dragon's side, near its hind legs. This area is generally safer from the frontal bite attacks and the direct line of sight for breath attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Managing the Tail Swipe">
    The dragon frequently uses a 180-degree tail swipe. Watch the boss's hips; when they shift, it indicates an incoming swipe. Use the block (F) or dash (Q) to avoid the hit. If you are a mobile class, dashing through the tail is often more effective than blocking.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Punishing Ground Slams">
    When the dragon rears up on its hind legs, it is preparing for a ground slam. Immediately dash backward. Once the shockwave passes, move back in for a heavy combo or a critical attack (R). This is the largest opening in the boss's rotation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Phase 2 Transitions">
    At 50% health, the boss will roar and its eyes will glow brighter. It will now chain attacks more frequently. Stay patient and do not over-commit to long attack strings. Use short M1 combos and keep at least 25% of your stamina reserved for emergency dodges.
  </Step>
</Steps>

For solo players, the key is "kiting." Use the Bowmaster class or magic skills to chip away at the boss's health while maintaining distance. If you must use melee, the "hit and run" tactic is mandatory. Land two to three hits, then dash away to observe the next move.

| Attack Name | Visual Cue | Recommended Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Bone Bite** | Head pulls back slightly | Dash to the side / Parry |
| **Tail Sweep** | Hips rotate outward | Block or Jump-Dash |
| **Great Slam** | Boss stands on hind legs | Dash backward immediately |
| **Roar** | Boss lowers its head | No damage, but prepares next move |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
Use the "Air Attack" (Jump + M1) to stay above the ground-slam shockwaves if your dash is on cooldown. This requires precise timing but can save you from a lethal blow.
</Callout>

</Section>
