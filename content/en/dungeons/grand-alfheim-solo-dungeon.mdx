<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Solo Route Walkthrough">

Navigating the Temple of Beginnings alone requires a deliberate approach. Running blindly into rooms will result in being surrounded by aggressive mobs, leading to rapid defeat. Follow this optimized pathing sequence to clear the dungeon efficiently.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Entrance Decision">
    Upon entering the dungeon lobby, you are faced with a choice between the left parkour route and the right combat route. For solo runs, take the left parkour route. Use your racial wings or precise jumping to navigate the floating stone pillars. This bypasses three entire groups of Scorpions and Dinosaurs, saving both your health pool and your run time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Miniboss Chamber">
    After crossing the parkour chasm, you will enter a stone courtyard where the Treant miniboss resides. If your level is below 12, do not engage the Treant directly. Hug the left wall closely to slip past its detection radius. If you choose to fight, keep the encounter in the center of the arena to avoid getting stuck on the surrounding root geometry.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Altar Cube Puzzle">
    To unlock the final boss portal, you must solve the angel and demon altar puzzle. Locate the two glowing stone cubes hidden in the side corridors. Pick up each cube and place them on their matching altars. Watch out for spawning plant enemies that guard the corridors; pull them one by one rather than fighting the entire group at once.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Portal Transition">
    Once both cubes are correctly placed, the central portal will activate with a bright cyan glow. Before stepping through, sit down or use healing consumables to restore your health and stamina to 100%. Verify that all your active skill cooldowns have fully reset.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Boss Mechanics and Solo Combat Tactics">

The final encounter against the Bone Dragon is where most solo attempts fail. Without teammates to distract the boss, you will be the target of every single attack string. Surviving this encounter requires memorizing the boss's visual cues and executing perfect defensive responses.

The Bone Dragon utilizes three primary attack patterns. The first is a frontal cone fire breath that deals continuous damage over time. The second is a heavy tail swipe that covers a wide 180-degree arc behind the boss. The third is a ground slam that releases a circular shockwave across the entire arena floor.

| Attack Name | Visual Cue | Danger Level | Solo Counter Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dragon Breath** | Blue flames gather in throat | Extreme | Dash immediately to the side of the neck |
| **Tail Swipe** | Boss twists its torso backward | High | Hold block (F key) or parry the strike |
| **Ground Slam** | Boss rears up on its hind legs | Medium | Jump over the expanding shockwave ring |
| **Biting Combo** | Rapid head snaps forward | High | Execute back-to-back parries to stagger |

```
[Bone Dragon Arena Positioning]
       
       ( Head / Breath Zone )  <-- DANGEROUS FRONT
              [ Boss ]
    ( Left )  /      \  ( Right ) <-- SAFEST ZONES TO ATTACK
             [  Tail  ]
                 |
       ( Tail Swipe Zone )    <-- DANGEROUS REAR (Block/Parry)
```

To defeat the dragon solo, always position yourself near its front claws rather than directly in front of its mouth or directly behind its tail. This positioning gives you ample time to react to both the breath attack and the tail swipe. When the dragon rears up for the ground slam, do not try to run away; instead, time your jump so that you are airborne when the shockwave passes beneath you.

<Callout type="tip" title="Parry Window Mastery">
Do not hold down the block button indefinitely. Holding block reduces your stamina regeneration rate. Instead, tap the block button (F key) exactly half a second before an attack lands to trigger a perfect parry. This negates all incoming damage and temporarily staggers the boss, creating a safe window for your heavy weapon combos.
</Callout>

</Section>
