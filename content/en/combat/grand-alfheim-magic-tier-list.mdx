<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Spell Progression Guide">

Unlocking your magic potential requires careful allocation of skill points. Follow this progression route to efficiently unlock and utilize your elemental spells.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Race & Weapon Class">
    Create your character and choose your race. Visit the Blacksmith in the starter town to select your weapon class (such as Swordsman or Gauntlets).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Acquire Skill Points">
    Complete mayor quests and hunt local mobs (like Boars and Scorpions) to level up your character and earn skill points for your trees.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Base Magic Spells">
    Open your Skill Tree menu. Allocate your first points into your elemental magic branch to unlock your primary projectile spell (e.g., Fireball).
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Equip and Hotkey Spells">
    Open your inventory, navigate to the skills tab, and assign your unlocked magic spells to your hotbar (Keys 1-4) for quick casting in combat.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Progression Milestone">
After unlocking your secondary magic skill (such as Flame Artillery), combine it with weapon skills like Sword Counter to execute devastating hybrid combos on bosses.
</Callout>
