Understanding how armor works in Grand Alfheim is crucial for surviving tough dungeons like the Temple of Beginnings. Unlike accessories that scale your health by percentages, armor sets provide flat health points (HP) to keep you alive during combat. 

**Video Highlights:**
- **Map Scale & Navigation**: The world map is massive, making early-game traversal a running simulator until you unlock gates.
- **Combat Core**: Successful combat relies on mastering parrying, heavy kicks, and managing your hunger bar.
- **Survival Cooking**: Starvation halts passive health regeneration; cooking raw meat via quick-time events is essential.

There are three main armor slots available for your character:
- **Mask** (Head slot, not included in all sets)
- **Top** (Chest armor piece)
- **Bottom** (Leg armor piece)

Additionally, players can purchase the **Advanced Armor** gamepass to unlock premium cosmetic skins. These skins overlay your equipped stat gear, allowing you to customize your look without losing valuable defensive attributes.

| Armor Feature | Flat HP Bonuses | Percentage Scaling | Cosmetic Overlay |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Standard Armor** | Yes | No | No |
| **Accessories** | No | Yes | No |
| **Advanced Armor** | No | No | Yes (Full Set Only) |

<Callout type="tip" title="Survival Strategy">

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Armor Progression Route">

To build up your defenses efficiently, follow this optimized gear progression path from level 1 to the endgame.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the Azure Knightplate">
    Run the Temple of Beginnings dungeon early. With a generous 20% drop rate, you can easily secure the Azure Knightplate Top and Bottom to gain a quick +245 HP boost.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm the Treant Boss">
    Once you reach Level 10, target the Treant Boss inside the Temple of Beginnings. Farm this boss until you drop the full Verdant Sovereign Set, which provides a massive jump to +598 HP.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Salamander Territory">
    Travel through the world gates to Salamander territory. Locate and defeat the Satyrs (0.1% drop rate) to obtain the Bloodcrown Regalia, giving you +1010 HP and a +3.5% Magic Damage bonus.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defeat the Unnamed Knight">
    For the ultimate defensive build, hunt down the Unnamed Knight at Level 25+ to obtain the Blackflame Knight Set, boosting your total pool by +1555 flat HP.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Build Optimization">
If your build relies heavily on spells or dark magic skills like Event Horizon, prioritize farming the Bloodcrown Regalia or Crimson Dragon sets for their Magic Damage (MD) multipliers.
</Callout>
