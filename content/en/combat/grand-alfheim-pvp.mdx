<Section icon={Target} title="Core Combat Mechanics & Combo Execution">

Succeeding in **grand alfheim pvp** requires mastering the game's intricate defensive and offensive control systems. Button-mashing will quickly lead to being parried and punished. You must learn to read your opponent's animations and manage your stamina bar efficiently.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Master the Parry (F Key)">
    Do not simply hold block. Tap the F key right before an incoming strike lands to trigger a parry, stunning your opponent and opening them up for a full combo.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Feints (M2 / Right Click)">
    Cancel your M1 attack animations mid-swing using M2. This baits out your opponent's block or parry, leaving them vulnerable to a critical strike.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute Dash Attacks (Q + M1)">
    Press Q to dash, then immediately press M1 to execute a rapid dash attack. This is the premier tool for closing distance or catching opponents off-guard.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Incorporate Air Attacks">
    Jump and swing to alter your attack timing. Air attacks help you bypass shield blocks and catch opponents who are preparing for ground-level parries.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Stamina Management">
Dashing, blocking, and executing heavy critical attacks (R key) deplete your stamina. If your stamina hits zero, you will enter a fatigued state, leaving you completely defenseless.
</Callout>

</Section>
