<Section icon={Map} title="Dungeon Drops: The Temple of Beginnings">

Once you reach Level 10, the focus of your **grand alfheim weapons** hunt shifts to the Temple of Beginnings. This is the first major milestone where common Blacksmith gear is replaced by Epic and Legendary variants. The dungeon features a unique branching path system: the left route focuses on parkour, while the right route is combat-heavy. Regardless of the path, you must solve cube puzzles to face the Bone Dragon and claim your rewards.

The weapons found here are significant upgrades, often featuring higher base damage and unique aesthetic glows. For example, the **Night's Edge** is the definitive upgrade for Swordsmen entering the mid-game, while the **Azure Reaver** offers superior reach for those tackling territory bosses.
