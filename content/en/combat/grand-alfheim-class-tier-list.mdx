<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Class Progression Guide">

To maximize your efficiency in Grand Alfheim, follow this structured progression path to level up your chosen class and unlock advanced special classes.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select a Solo-Friendly Starter">
    Begin with a high-survivability class like a Knight or a high-damage Mage to make early boar quests and dungeon clearing easier.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate the Starter Quests">
    Complete the Mayor's introductory quests. If the quest chain loops or an NPC like the Librarian disappears, log out and log back in immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate the Blacksmith">
    Travel to the main towns on Leprechaun Island or Gnome Island to locate the blacksmith and experiment with different archetypes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Farm Starter Dungeons">
    Run the Temple of Beginnings repeatedly to level up your skills, acquire better weapons, and gather materials for class upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Unlock Special Classes">
    Conquer end-game dungeons like Ode to the Abyss to find rare drops and unlock elite archetypes such as the Trident class.
  </Step>
</Steps>
