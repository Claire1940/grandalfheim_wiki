<Section icon={Target} title="How to Change and Unlock Classes">

One of the best features of Grand Alfheim is the ability to swap your build without restarting your character. This allows you to adapt to new updates or try out a legendary weapon drop you just found in a dungeon.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate a Blacksmith">
    Travel to a major hub like the **Salamander City** or **Undine Village**. Look for the anvil icon on your map.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Interact with the Forge">
    Talk to the Blacksmith NPC. You will see a list of the 11 standard classes. Changing classes is currently **free of charge**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Equip Your New Weapon">
    Upon selecting a class, the Blacksmith will provide a basic version of that weapon. Ensure you equip it in your inventory (B key) to activate the class skills.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reset Skill Trees (Optional)">
    If you are switching from a Magic-heavy build to a Strength-heavy build, visit the **Token Merchant** to reset your skill points for 100,000 Gold or Robux.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Dungeon Unlocks">
Special classes like the **Trident** cannot be obtained at the Blacksmith. These require specific "Class Tomes" that drop exclusively from the **Temple of Beginnings** legendary chests with a sub-1% drop rate.
</Callout>

</Section>

To maximize the potential of the **grand alfheim best class**, you must align your stats with your weapon's scaling. Investing points randomly will lead to a "dead build" that struggles in the later islands like **Gnome** or **Leprechaun**.
