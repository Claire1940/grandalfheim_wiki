<Section icon={Target} title="Fisherman Bubba Questline Walkthrough">

Once you arrive at the **grand alfheim fisherman bubba location**, interacting with him will initiate the basic fishing questline. This series of tasks introduces you to the fishing minigame, which provides a steady stream of gold and useful cooking ingredients.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain the Starter Rod">
    Interact with Fisherman Bubba and select the dialogue option to ask about fishing. He will hand you a **Basic Fishing Rod** for free.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Catch Starter Fish">
    Equip your rod from the inventory menu (**M** key). Stand near the edge of the wooden pier and cast your line into the glowing water ripples. Catch 3 common fish to satisfy Bubba's first request.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Deliver the Catch">
    Talk to Bubba again to hand over the fish. He will reward you with early-game experience points, gold, and a handful of specialized bait.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Stamina Management">
Fishing consumes a small amount of stamina per cast. Ensure you do not deplete your stamina completely, as you will need it to dash or run if aggressive local wildlife wanders near the docks.
</Callout>

</Section>
