<Section icon={Target} title="How to Use the Hunting Board Effectively">

Interacting with a hunting board is straightforward, but maximizing the returns requires a tactical approach. You should never wander into the wilderness without an active bounty. The rewards from these boards often include rare materials used by the Blacksmith to craft your first set of Iron or Bone equipment.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Hub Board">
    Travel to your race's capital city. Use the **V** key to fly if you are in a vertical territory like Sylph or Undine to spot the board from the air.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Filter by Difficulty">
    Open the board interface. Tasks are color-coded: **White** for Common, **Green** for Uncommon, and **Blue** for Rare. Always prioritize Blue tasks if your level permits.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Sync with Main Quests">
    Check your quest log (**M** key). If your main story quest requires you to visit the Scorpions near the Temple of Beginnings, select the "Scorpion Sting" bounty from the board.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute and Turn In">
    Complete the objectives in the field. Unlike some modern MMOs, most boards in Grand Alfheim require you to return to the board or a specific NPC to claim your rewards.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Bounty Limits">
You can typically only hold **three active hunting board tasks** at a time. If you find a higher-tier bounty, you may need to abandon a lower-tier one to make room.
</Callout>

The **grand alfheim hunting board location** in the Undine territory is particularly popular for group play. Because Undine players often focus on healing and support, they can pick up "Elite" bounties that are nearly impossible to solo, then find a party in the Azure Plaza to clear them for massive XP bonuses.

</Section>
