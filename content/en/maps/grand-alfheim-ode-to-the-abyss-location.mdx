<Section icon={Swords} title="Step-by-Step Raid Guide for Valdrayn">

Defeating Valdrayn requires strict coordination. The fight is split into distinct survival and burn phases. Below is the step-by-step strategy to successfully clear the encounter.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Lower the Boss's Health">
    Engage Valdrayn in the center of the arena. Tanks (Aegis class) should hold aggro while DPS classes (Titanblade, Assassin, Bowmaster) attack from behind to avoid direct frontal sweeps.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prepare for the Tower Phase">
    When Valdrayn reaches low health, he will shield himself and activate **four towers** around the arena. He will begin regenerating health rapidly until all four towers are destroyed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Split and Destroy Towers">
    Immediately split your party into pre-assigned pairs or solo players to target each of the four towers. Save your high-damage, high-cooldown skills specifically for this phase to break them quickly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Final Burn">
    Once the towers shatter, Valdrayn's shield will drop, leaving him temporarily vulnerable. Group back up and unleash all remaining attacks to finish the boss before he can restart the cycle.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Tower Allocation">
Assign your highest single-target burst classes, like the Assassin or Duelist, to the furthest towers. Use your mobility dashes (Q key on PC) to close the distance instantly.
</Callout>

</Section>
